Check-List structure

1. Title - title or description of the checklist.

2. Date - the date the checklist was created or last used.

3. Task List - A list of tasks to be completed.

4. Expected result - describes what the final results of a task or process should look like after all the tasks in the checklist have been completed

5. Comments fields - here you can leave notes about the problems that arose during the execution of tasks, or describe what exactly was completed.
